About us

Our path

Established in 2002, Luigi’s started off as a delivery and take away pizza place. Over the years Luigi’s has expanded to become a full fledged pizzeria offering its customers the opportunity to dine in and eat hot, out of the oven pizzas. Luigi’s menu has also expanded to offer Salads, Chicken Wings, Soups and Pastas. Luigi’s now offers a Gluten free option on all its menu items. With two delivery cars, our delivery service is prompt.
